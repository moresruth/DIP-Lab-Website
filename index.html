Development Innovations and Policy Laboratory
- DIP Lab

The Center for Strategic Planning and Policy Studies (CSPPS) is one of the three units of the College of Public Affairs and Development (CPAf) focused on generating timely, objective, and science-based policy research programs concerning public policy and decision making.

Recognizing the huge amount of underutilized research evidence in the AANR sector and the need to strengthen the capacities of policy actors and stakeholders, CSPSS took the lead to establish the DIP Lab in 2022.

Our Services

POLICY DATABASE

The DIP Lab database is a digital repository of policy-related knowledge products in the AANR sector. It is is readily accessible to both the public and experts and serves as the basis for preparing information packs and facilitating policy foresight activities of the lab.

CAPACITY BUILDING

Innovative training activities on strategic planning and policy analysis are conducted in partnership with our in- house experts at the College of Public Affairs and Development (CPAf) and specialists in and around the UP system.

The DIP Lab provides improved learning modules catered to the needs of different participants in the academe, national government agencies, research and development institutions, local government units, and other groups.

PHYSICAL FACILITY

The DIP Lab's state-of-the-art learning and innovation facility is housed at the CPAf Building in UPLB. Activities include Policy Hackathons, Policy Round Table Discussions (RTDs), and Role Playing/Simulation Games.

It is equipped with large-screen computers, projectors, and other audio-visual learning equipment for use during face-to-face and virtual policy design co-creation and service-learning activities.

POLICY-RELEVANT MATERIALS & MEDIA CONTENT

We produce policy briefs, reports, infographics, and a podcast program to ensure that relevant information are communicated to the public.

The DIP Lab also extends its services online through Facebook, Twitter, and LinkedIn.

POLICY ANALYSIS AND ADVOCACY PROJECTS

Virtual and small face-to-face workshops for project proponents and stakeholders take place in the DIP Lab.

We offer support in the whole advocacy process such as in the stakeholders’ consultation and analysis, consensus building, and translating research results into advocacy agenda.
